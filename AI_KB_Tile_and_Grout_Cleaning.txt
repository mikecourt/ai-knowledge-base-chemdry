================================================================================

WHAT IS TILE AND GROUT CLEANING?
The Tile, Stone & Grout Cleaning service from Chem-Dry restores the beauty and
cleanliness of tile and grout surfaces throughout the home. Using specialized
equipment and cleaning solutions, this service removes deeply embedded dirt,
grime, and bacteria that regular mopping can't reach.

KEY BENEFITS:
‚Ä¢ Removes deeply embedded dirt and grime from porous grout
‚Ä¢ Restores original brightness and color to grout lines
‚Ä¢ Eliminates bacteria, mold, and mildew in grout
‚Ä¢ Professional equipment reaches what mopping can't
‚Ä¢ Optional sealing protects grout from future staining
‚Ä¢ Extends the life of tile and grout investment
‚Ä¢ Healthier, more hygienic floors
‚Ä¢ Enhanced appearance of entire room

COMMON APPLICATIONS:
‚Ä¢ Kitchen floors and backsplashes
‚Ä¢ Bathroom floors, showers, and tub surrounds
‚Ä¢ Entryways and mudrooms
‚Ä¢ Laundry rooms
‚Ä¢ Sunrooms and enclosed porches
‚Ä¢ Commercial facilities (restaurants, retail, offices)

WHY GROUT GETS DIRTY:
Grout is porous and absorbs:
‚Ä¢ Dirt and soil tracked in from outside
‚Ä¢ Spills and stains from cooking, bathing, etc.
‚Ä¢ Mop water (which spreads dirt around)
‚Ä¢ Oils and residues from cleaning products
‚Ä¢ Moisture that promotes mold/mildew growth

Regular mopping only cleans the surface - dirt embeds deep in grout pores,
causing discoloration and deterioration over time.

4. TILE AND GROUT SCIENCE
================================================================================

WHY PROFESSIONAL CLEANING IS NECESSARY:

GROUT POROSITY:
‚Ä¢ Grout is cement-based and highly porous
‚Ä¢ Pores absorb dirt, oils, moisture, and stains
‚Ä¢ Surface cleaning (mopping) doesn't reach embedded soil
‚Ä¢ Over time, embedded dirt causes permanent discoloration

LIMITATIONS OF MOPPING:
‚Ä¢ Spreads dirty water across surface
‚Ä¢ Pushes soil deeper into grout pores
‚Ä¢ Leaves residue that attracts more dirt
‚Ä¢ Cannot generate enough pressure to extract embedded soil
‚Ä¢ Often uses incorrect pH cleaners that damage grout

PROFESSIONAL ADVANTAGES:
‚Ä¢ High-pressure extraction reaches deep into pores
‚Ä¢ Specialized solutions break down embedded oils and soil
‚Ä¢ Hot water enhances cleaning effectiveness
‚Ä¢ Powerful vacuum extraction removes dirty water completely
‚Ä¢ No dirt-attracting residue left behind
‚Ä¢ Appropriate solutions for each tile/grout type

THE IMPORTANCE OF SEALING:
Unsealed grout acts like a sponge:
‚Ä¢ Absorbs spills and stains immediately
‚Ä¢ Provides environment for mold/mildew growth
‚Ä¢ Deteriorates faster from moisture penetration
‚Ä¢ Requires more frequent professional cleaning

Sealed grout creates a protective barrier:
‚Ä¢ Repels liquids and prevents stain absorption
‚Ä¢ Inhibits mold and mildew growth
‚Ä¢ Easier to maintain with routine mopping
‚Ä¢ Extends grout lifespan significantly
‚Ä¢ Maintains clean appearance longer between professional cleanings

NATURAL STONE CONSIDERATIONS:
Stone surfaces require special care:
‚Ä¢ More porous than ceramic/porcelain
‚Ä¢ Can be damaged by acidic or harsh cleaners
‚Ä¢ May require lower pressure cleaning
‚Ä¢ Often need specialized stone sealers
‚Ä¢ Professional cleaning prevents etching and damage

6. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

SERVICE QUESTIONS:

Q: How long does the service take?
A: Most tile and grout cleaning jobs take 1-3 hours depending on size and
condition. A small bathroom might take 45-60 minutes, while a large kitchen
could take 2-3 hours. We'll give you a more accurate timeframe after assessing
your specific situation. If you're adding grout sealing, that adds about 30-45
minutes plus drying time.

Q: How often should I have my tile and grout professionally cleaned?
A: For most homes, we recommend professional cleaning every 1-2 years. High-
traffic areas (kitchen, entryway) may benefit from annual cleaning. If you have
sealed grout, you can extend to every 2 years. Commercial facilities typically
need quarterly or semi-annual cleaning.

Q: Will the cleaning damage my tile or grout?
A: Absolutely not. Our technicians are trained to use appropriate pressure and
solutions for each tile type. We test questionable areas before starting and
adjust our methods for delicate surfaces like natural stone. Our process is
safe and effective for all tile and grout types.

Q: Can you remove all stains from my grout?
A: We can remove most stains, but some deeply set stains (especially in very
old, unsealed grout) may be permanent. In these cases, we offer color sealing
which changes the grout color to a fresh, uniform appearance while providing
maximum protection.

Q: Do I need to seal my grout?
A: We highly recommend it. Sealing creates a protective barrier that prevents
stains, moisture damage, and mold growth. It makes routine cleaning much easier
and extends the time between professional cleanings. For the small additional
cost, it provides significant long-term value.

Q: How long before I can walk on the floor after cleaning?
A: For cleaning only, floors are typically dry enough to walk on within 1-2
hours. If you add grout sealing, we recommend waiting 24-48 hours before heavy
traffic to allow the sealer to cure fully. Light foot traffic is fine after a
few hours.

Q: Can you clean shower walls and tub surrounds?
A: Yes! Vertical surfaces require specialized techniques, and pricing is
typically higher than floors due to the additional difficulty. We clean shower
walls, tub surrounds, backsplashes, and any tiled vertical surface.

Q: What's the difference between standard sealing and color sealing?
A: Standard sealing creates a clear protective barrier over your existing grout
color. It prevents stains and moisture but doesn't change the appearance. Color
sealing applies a pigmented sealer that changes the grout color to your choice
(often a uniform neutral tone), covering existing stains while providing
maximum protection. Color sealing is more expensive but gives a "like new"
appearance.

PRICING & VALUE QUESTIONS:

Q: Why is professional tile cleaning so much more expensive than DIY?
A: Professional equipment costs $15,000-30,000 and provides far superior
results. Our specialized solutions are designed for tile and grout (not generic
cleaners), our technicians have years of training, and you get guaranteed
results. Most importantly, our cleaning lasts much longer - you'll actually
save money over time compared to repeatedly renting equipment and spending your
weekends scrubbing.

Q: Do you charge by the room or by square footage?
A: We price by square footage, which ensures you only pay for what we actually
clean. A "room" can vary dramatically in size, so square footage is more fair
and transparent. We measure on-site to provide accurate pricing.

Q: Is there a minimum charge?
A: Most franchises have a minimum service charge (typically $75-150) to cover
travel time and setup costs. This is waived for larger jobs. Ask about bundling
with other services (carpet cleaning, upholstery) to maximize value.

Q: Are there any hidden fees?
A: No. We provide transparent pricing after measuring your space. The only
additional charges would be for services you request (like grout sealing) which
we discuss upfront. There are no surprise fees.

Q: Do you offer any guarantees?
A: Yes! All Chem-Dry services come with a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not
happy with the results, let us know within 30 days and we'll make it right.
That might mean re-cleaning specific areas or working with you to resolve any
concerns.

PROCESS & PREPARATION QUESTIONS:

Q: Do I need to move furniture?
A: We ask that you remove small items, rugs, and easily movable furniture. For
heavy furniture (refrigerators, stoves, washers), we can work around them or
help move them if needed. Just let us know in advance.

Q: What do I need to do to prepare?
A: Clear floor surfaces of loose items, secure pets in a separate area, and
make sure we have access to water and electricity. Remove any rugs or mats from
tile areas. That's it - we handle the rest!

Q: Will there be any odor or fumes?
A: Our cleaning solutions are low-odor and safe. You may notice a mild, clean
scent during and immediately after service, but it dissipates quickly. We don't
use harsh chemicals with strong fumes.

Q: Can you clean while I'm not home?
A: Some customers prefer this arrangement. We'd need access to the home and
clear communication about what areas to clean. Most people prefer to be present
for the initial walkthrough and final inspection, but we're flexible.

Q: What if I have pets?
A: Our cleaning solutions are pet-safe once dry. During service, we ask that
you secure pets in a separate area for their safety and to allow our
technicians to work efficiently. Pets can return to cleaned areas once floors
are dry.

RESULTS & MAINTENANCE QUESTIONS:

Q: How can I maintain my tile and grout between professional cleanings?
A:
‚Ä¢ Use a pH-neutral tile cleaner (not acidic or alkaline)
‚Ä¢ Sweep or vacuum regularly to prevent dirt buildup
‚Ä¢ Wipe up spills immediately
‚Ä¢ Damp mop (don't flood the floor)
‚Ä¢ Change mop water frequently
‚Ä¢ Avoid oil-based or waxy cleaners
‚Ä¢ Consider annual re-sealing

Q: Will my grout return to its original color?
A: In most cases, yes! Professional cleaning removes the embedded dirt causing
discoloration. However, very old grout or grout that's been deeply stained for
many years may have permanent discoloration. Color sealing is an excellent
option for these situations.

Q: How long will the cleaning results last?
A: With proper maintenance, most customers find results last 1-2 years. Sealed
grout stays cleaner longer (2-3 years). High-traffic areas may show soiling
sooner. Regular sweeping and proper mopping significantly extend the life of
professional cleaning.

Q: Can you remove mold and mildew from shower grout?
A: Yes. Our cleaning process kills mold and mildew while removing visible
staining. For chronic mold problems, we recommend grout sealing after cleaning
to prevent moisture penetration. In some cases, improving bathroom ventilation
is also important.
